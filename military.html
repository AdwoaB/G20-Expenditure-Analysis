This page draws insight into the military spending of the G20. The analysis below includes the military spending to the country's GDP, military GDP per capita compared to total GDP per capita, as well as the military-to-education allocation of funds and military-to-health allocation of funds. The data used for this analysis was acquired from the World Bank's Official Website and it contains performance indicators of the G20 from 2010 - 2016.
